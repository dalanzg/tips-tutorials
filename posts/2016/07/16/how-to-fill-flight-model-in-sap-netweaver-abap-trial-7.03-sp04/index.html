How to fill flight model in SAP Netweaver ABAP Trial 7.03 SP04

Published on July 16, 2016 by Daniel Lanza

 sap

This tutorial will explain how to fill flight model in SAP Netweaver.

TableDescriptionKeys
T000Clientclient
SCURXCurrenciescurrency
SBUSPARTBusiness partnerclient, partner number
STRAVELAGTravel agenciesclient, travel agency number
SCUSTOMCustomersclient, customer number
SCARRCarriersclient, carrier ID
SCOUNTERSales countersclient, carrier ID, sales counter number
SPFLIFlight scheduleclient, carrier ID, connection number
SFLIGHTFlightsclient, carrier ID, connection number, date of flight
SBOOKFlight bookingsclient, carrier ID, connection number, date of flight, booking number, customer number
